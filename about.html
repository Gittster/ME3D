About M&E 3D

Passionate about 3D printing and bringing creative ideas to life

Our Story

M&E 3D Designs started with a simple passion for creating. Based in Otsego, Minnesota, we saw the incredible potential of 3D printing technology to turn ideas into tangible reality.

What began as a hobby quickly grew into a mission: making high-quality 3D printing accessible to everyone. Whether it's a custom gift, a functional household item, or a unique piece of art, we love helping our customers bring their visions to life.

Today, we serve customers across the Twin Cities area and beyond, delivering quality prints with fast turnaround times and personalized service that larger companies can't match.

Our Process
